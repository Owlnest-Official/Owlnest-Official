Collection FAQ

Why are there two prices for Owlnest Lume?

The standard MSRP remains $119, while current campaign pricing is reserved for the crowdfunding campaign. This separates long-term retail pricing from limited campaign offers for early backers.

Is this just a warm night light?

No. Ordinary night lights are usually built for visibility or decoration. Owlnest Lume is designed specifically for nighttime wind-down and the final 1 to 2 hours before bed.

When should I use it?

Owlnest Lume is designed for the 1 to 2 hours before bed, when you want the room to move away from daytime lighting and toward a calmer evening atmosphere.

Where do I actually back or purchase it?

Backing currently happens through the Indiegogo campaign, not through direct website checkout. Use the campaign CTAs on this site to continue.

What is the correct light range?

The correct Owlnest Lume range is 1500K-1800K. That is the consistent specification used across the current site and manual.

Is it a medical device?

No. Owlnest Lume is a science-informed environmental product for nighttime wind-down. It is not a medical device and is not presented as a treatment or cure for sleep disorders.

What are the warranty and return terms?

Campaign-backed units are expected to include a standard 1-year limited warranty for manufacturing defects. Refund timing and fulfillment details follow the current campaign stage rather than normal instant-retail checkout rules.
