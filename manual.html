How to use Owlnest Lume

1. Place Owlnest Lume in your bedside area or another part of the room where it can support your evening environment without acting as bright task lighting.

2. Turn it on during the last 1 to 2 hours before bed, especially when you want the room to feel calmer and less like daytime.

3. Use it to replace brighter overhead light, harsh bedside lamps, or other lighting that keeps the room feeling active late at night.

4. Owlnest Lume is best used as a repeatable environmental cue for nighttime wind-down, not as a promise of instant sleep results.

What Owlnest Lume is and is not

What it is
  • A compact circadian rhythm lamp.
  • A nighttime wind-down tool for the bedroom or evening routine.
  • A product designed around 1500K–1800K amber light and lower-stimulation nighttime use.
What it is not
  • Not a generic night light for simple visibility or navigation.
  • Not a medical device and not a treatment for insomnia or any sleep disorder.
  • Not a sunrise alarm, white noise machine, or app-based sleep gadget.

Safety and care

Use only compatible charging accessories and follow normal battery-safety precautions.

Do not disassemble the lamp or expose charging components to moisture.

Clean with a soft dry cloth. Avoid harsh solvents or abrasive cleaning materials.

Keep the product away from extreme heat, prolonged direct water exposure, and situations where it may be crushed or sharply bent.
